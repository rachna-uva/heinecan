Tackling climate change is no easy feat.

Achieving net-zero carbon emissions gives us a sustainable future, but with carbon building up so rapidly in the atmosphere, the need to draw up more accurate carbon budgets is increasing.

The Global Carbon Project combines information about greenhouse gases, including carbon dioxide and methane. The international science community contributes to this project by analyzing natural and human-driven factors for climate change to develop a complete picture of the global carbon cycle.
